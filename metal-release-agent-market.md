<p><p>The metal release agent market is divided into two primary types: permanent and semi-permanent agents. Permanent release agents are designed for extended use, providing long-lasting separation between the metal part and the mold, ideal for high-volume production. Semi-permanent agents, on the other hand, require reapplication after a certain number of cycles, offering flexibility for varying production needs. Both types enhance efficiency and reduce defects in manufacturing processes, catering to specific industry demands for quality and performance.</p></p>
