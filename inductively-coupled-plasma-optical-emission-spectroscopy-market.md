<p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma-Optical Emission Spectroscopy (ICP-OES) markets can be categorized into sequential and simultaneous types. Sequential ICP-OES analyzes elements one after another, providing high sensitivity for trace elements, while allowing users to identify specific wavelengths. Simultaneous ICP-OES, on the other hand, measures multiple elements concurrently, enhancing throughput and efficiency. Both types cater to different analytical needs, with sequential types favored for complex samples requiring precise detection, while simultaneous types excel in high-production environments where rapid results are essential.</p></p>
