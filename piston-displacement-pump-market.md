<p><ul><li>Lift Pump</li><li>Force Pump</li><li>Axial Piston Pump</li><li>Radial Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piston displacement pump market includes various types, each serving distinct applications. Lift pumps function by creating a vacuum to move fluids vertically, often used in water wells. Force pumps utilize pressure to push fluids through a system, ideal for high-viscosity materials. Axial piston pumps employ a series of pistons aligned parallel to the drive shaft, providing high efficiency in hydraulic systems. In contrast, radial piston pumps feature pistons arranged radially, offering robust performance for heavy-duty applications. Together, they cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
