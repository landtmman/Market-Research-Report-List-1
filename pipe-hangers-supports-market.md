Pipe hangers are devices that suspend pipes from ceilings or structures, ensuring proper alignment and load distribution. Pipe supports provide stability at points where pipes rest on a surface, preventing sagging or movement. Both categories are essential for maintaining system integrity, facilitating maintenance, and accommodating thermal expansion and contraction in industrial, commercial, and residential applications, ensuring efficient and safe operation of piping networks.</p></p>
