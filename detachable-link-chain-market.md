<p><p>The detachable link chain market is divided into two primary types: metal chains and non-metal chains. Metal chains, often made from steel or other alloys, are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications like lifting and towing. Non-metal chains, typically composed of materials like plastic or nylon, offer advantages such as lightweight and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for lighter loads and environments sensitive to metal corrosion.
