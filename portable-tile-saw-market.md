<p><ul><li>Wet Saw</li><li>Dry Saw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable tile saw market is primarily divided into two types: wet saws and dry saws. Wet saws utilize water to cool the cutting blade, minimizing dust and providing cleaner cuts, making them suitable for ceramic and porcelain tiles. Dry saws, on the other hand, operate without water, generating more dust but offering greater portability and ease of use for quick jobs. Each type caters to specific user needs, with wet saws favored for precision and dry saws appreciated for convenience.</p></p>
