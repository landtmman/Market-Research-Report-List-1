<p><p>The contour and surface measuring machine market is divided into contact and non-contact types. Contact contour and surface measuring machines utilize physical probes to precisely measure surface profiles and geometries by making direct contact with the object's surface. In contrast, non-contact contour and surface measuring machines employ optical or laser technologies to capture surface features without physical interaction. Each type serves specific applications, with contact machines being ideal for detailed measurements, while non-contact machines offer speed and the ability to assess delicate surfaces.</p></p>
