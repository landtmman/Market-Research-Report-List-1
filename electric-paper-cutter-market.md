<p><p>The electric paper cutter market is divided into semi-automatic and automatic types. Semi-automatic cutters require some manual input, allowing operators to control speed and alignment while benefiting from powered cutting blades. In contrast, automatic cutters offer fully automated operations, where the machine handles paper positioning, cutting, and stacking for increased efficiency and precision. Both types cater to varying production needs, with semi-automatic models suitable for smaller tasks, while automatic models are ideal for high-volume, continuous cutting applications in commercial settings.</p></p>
