<p><p>Dual port wall chargers come in three primary types: USB-A, USB-C, and USB-Type C. USB-A chargers feature the traditional rectangular connector, widely compatible with older devices. USB-C chargers offer a reversible design, supporting faster charging and data transfer for modern gadgets. USB-Type C, often used interchangeably with USB-C, includes enhanced features like Power Delivery for higher power output. The market is driven by the increasing demand for versatile and efficient charging solutions as users adopt more smart devices.</p></p>
