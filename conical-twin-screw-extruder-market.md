<p><p>The Conical Twin Screw Extruder market comprises two main types: co-rotating and counter-rotating extruders. Co-rotating extruders feature screws that rotate in the same direction, enhancing mixing and material processing efficiency, making them suitable for various polymer applications. In contrast, counter-rotating extruders have screws rotating in opposite directions, providing better mineral and bulk material handling. Both types cater to specific requirements in the plastics, food, and pharmaceutical industries, offering distinct advantages based on the application needs.</p></p>
