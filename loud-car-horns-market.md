<p><ul><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The loud car horns market can be categorized into three main types: air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns use compressed air to produce a loud sound, typically found in trucks and emergency vehicles. Electronic horns rely on electrical impulses to create sound, offering versatile designs and pitch options, often used in passenger cars. Electromagnetic horns utilize electromagnetic mechanisms to generate sound, providing a loud, clear tone ideal for various applications, including automotive and marine uses.</p></p>
