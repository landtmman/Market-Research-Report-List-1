<p><strong>The Bluetooth Trackers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radio Frequency Trackers</li><li>BLE Bluetooth Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth tracker market primarily includes two main types: Radio Frequency (RF) trackers and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) tags. RF trackers operate on specific radio frequencies, providing ranges up to several hundred feet, suitable for locating items over longer distances. In contrast, BLE tags leverage low-energy Bluetooth technology, enabling efficient battery use and seamless integration with smartphones. BLE tags typically have a shorter range but offer enhanced functionality, such as smartphone alerts and geofencing capabilities, making them popular for everyday item tracking.</p></p>
