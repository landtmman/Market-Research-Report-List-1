<p><p>Walking rotary cultivators are essential gardening tools available in three main types: corded electric, fuel-powered, and battery-powered. Corded electric models rely on a power outlet, providing consistent power for larger areas but limiting mobility. Fuel-powered cultivators use gasoline engines, offering high power and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks but requiring more maintenance. Battery-powered versions offer convenience and portability, enabling use in various locations without cords or fumes, though they may have limited runtime compared to other options.</p></p>
