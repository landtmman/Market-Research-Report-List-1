<p><p>The asbestos apron market is divided into two primary types: dust-free and dust-laden. Dust-free aprons are designed to minimize the release of asbestos fibers, providing a safer option for environments where exposure risk is high, such as in asbestos removal or contamination zones. In contrast, dust-laden aprons are typically used in more hazardous settings where asbestos fibers are prevalent, offering basic protective barriers but posing higher risks for the wearers due to potential fiber release during handling or movement.</p></p>
