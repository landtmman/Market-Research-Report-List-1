<p><p>The thermal annealing system for the semiconductor market includes two primary types: Flash Lamp Annealing (FLA) and Laser Thermal Annealing (LTA). FLA uses intense bursts of light to rapidly heat and cool semiconductor materials, enhancing electrical properties and repairing damage from previous processing. In contrast, LTA employs focused laser beams to achieve localized heating, allowing for precise control over temperature profiles and minimizing thermal stress. Both methods are vital for optimizing semiconductor fabrication and improving device performance.</p></p>
