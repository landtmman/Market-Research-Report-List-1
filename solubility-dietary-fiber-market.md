<p><p>The solubility dietary fiber market encompasses various types, each with distinct properties and applications. Cellulose, a non-soluble fiber, provides structural support in plants and aids digestion. Hemicellulose, often soluble, enhances gut health and is found in plant cell walls. Chitin and chitosan, derived from shellfish, are used for their health benefits, including weight management and cholesterol reduction. Other fibers, like psyllium and inulin, offer diverse functionalities for food and supplements, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
