<p><p>The electric stacker truck market consists of various types, primarily categorized into semi-electric stackers and fully electric stackers. Semi-electric stackers are equipped with an electric motor for lifting but require manual effort for propulsion, making them cost-effective and suitable for lighter tasks. In contrast, fully electric stackers offer complete electric operation for both lifting and movement, providing enhanced efficiency and ease of use, especially in larger operations. This differentiation caters to diverse warehousing and material handling needs across various industries.</p></p>
