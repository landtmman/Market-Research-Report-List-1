<p><strong>The Electrical Cord Reels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Retractable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical cord reels market comprises two primary types: manual and retractable. Manual cord reels require users to manually wind and unwind the electrical cord, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for low-frequency usage. In contrast, retractable cord reels feature a spring-loaded mechanism that automatically retracts the cord when not in use, providing convenience and reducing tangling. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with manual reels typically favored for basic applications and retractable reels preferred for frequent use in various environments.</p></p>
