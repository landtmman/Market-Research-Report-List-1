<p><p>The belt-drive turntable market encompasses three primary cartridge types: Moving Magnetic (MM), Moving Iron (MI), and Moving Coil (MC). Moving Magnetic cartridges use a magnet fixed to the stylus, providing good sound quality at a lower cost. Moving Iron cartridges employ a magnet in the body, allowing for a unique design and sound profile. Moving Coil cartridges deliver superior audio fidelity through a lightweight coil, but are typically more expensive and require specific phono preamps. Each type caters to diverse audiophile preferences and budgets.</p></p>
