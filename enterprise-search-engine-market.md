<p><p>The Enterprise Search Engine market comprises two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based search engines are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced maintenance for organizations. They allow users to access information from anywhere with an internet connection. In contrast, on-premises search engines are installed and managed within a company's own infrastructure, providing enhanced control over data security and customization.
