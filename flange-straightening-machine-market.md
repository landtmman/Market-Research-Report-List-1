<p><p>The flange straightening machine market comprises primarily two types: H-beam flange straightening machines and hydraulic flange straightening machines. H-beam flange straightening machines are specifically designed to straighten H-beams, ensuring accurate profiles and dimensions essential for structural applications. In contrast, hydraulic flange straightening machines utilize hydraulic pressure to effectively straighten various flange types, offering versatility for different materials and thicknesses. Both types play critical roles in metal fabrication, enhancing efficiency and product quality in construction and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
