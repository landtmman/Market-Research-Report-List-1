<p><p>The bottle conveyor market is categorized into automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic conveyors are fully mechanized, allowing for high-speed, continuous operation with minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, semi-automatic conveyors require some manual handling, combining automated processes with operator involvement. This type is suitable for businesses with moderate production needs or those transitioning to full automation. Both options enhance efficiency in bottling operations across various industries, including beverages and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
