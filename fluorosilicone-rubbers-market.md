<p><p>Fluorosilicone rubbers are categorized into two main market types: High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV). HTV fluorosilicone rubbers are processed at elevated temperatures, offering superior heat resistance and durability, making them ideal for extreme environments. Conversely, RTV fluorosilicone rubbers cure at ambient temperatures, providing ease of application and versatility for various sealing and bonding applications. Both types serve distinct industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, due to their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
