<p><p>Industrial Remote Terminal Units (RTUs) are essential for remote monitoring and control in various industries. The market consists of two primary types: Wireless Industrial RTUs and Wired Industrial RTUs. Wireless Industrial RTUs utilize radio frequency, cellular, or satellite communication, enabling flexible installation and reducing cable infrastructure costs. In contrast, Wired Industrial RTUs connect through physical cables, offering reliable and secure data transmission. Each type caters to specific operational needs, influenced by factors such as location, budget, and required communication reliability.</p></p>
