<p><p>The neurovascular devices industry encompasses various specialized products designed for treating vascular conditions in the brain. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to occlude aneurysms, preventing rupture. Cerebral balloon angioplasty and stenting systems open blocked arteries, restoring blood flow. Support devices, such as guidewires and microcatheters, assist in procedures, enhancing precision. Neurothrombectomy devices aim to remove blood clots from cerebral vessels, improving outcomes for stroke patients. Together, these devices play a crucial role in neurovascular intervention and patient care.</p></p>
