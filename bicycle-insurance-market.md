<p><strong>The Bicycle Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Third-Party Insurance Policy</li><li>Standalone own-damage Insurance Policy</li><li>Comprehensive Insurance Policy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle insurance market offers three main types of policies. Third-party insurance covers damages to others in case of an accident, providing liability protection. Standalone own-damage insurance focuses on covering repairs or losses to the policyholder's bicycle, excluding third-party liabilities. Comprehensive insurance combines both, offering extensive coverage for third-party damages and own-damage incidents, as well as additional features like theft or natural disaster protection. Each type caters to different needs and risk preferences of cyclists.</p></p>
