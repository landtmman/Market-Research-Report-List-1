<p><p>Hybrid cloud computing encompasses various market types, including Platform as a Service (PaaS), Software as a Service (SaaS), and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS). PaaS provides a platform for developers to build, deploy, and manage applications without worrying about underlying infrastructure. SaaS delivers software applications over the internet on a subscription basis, eliminating the need for installation. IaaS offers virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing businesses to scale infrastructure as needed while maintaining flexibility across public and private clouds.</p></p>
