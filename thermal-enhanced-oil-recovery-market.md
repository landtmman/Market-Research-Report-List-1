<p><p>Thermal Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) includes several market types aimed at improving oil extraction efficiency. Polymers are used to increase viscosity and improve sweep efficiency. Surfactants reduce surface tension, enhancing oil mobilization. Alkaline chemicals help in soap formation, improving oil displacement. Foamers create stable foams that enhance oil recovery in low permeability formations. Other substances may include solvents and various chemical agents that facilitate the recovery process, optimizing production and reducing operational costs in mature oil fields.</p></p>
