<p><ul><li>Core Switch</li><li>Distribution Switches</li><li>Access Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the data center switch market, three main types are essential: Core switches, Distribution switches, and Access switches. Core switches serve as the backbone, providing high-speed connectivity between data center devices. Distribution switches aggregate data from Access switches and manage traffic between different network segments. Access switches connect end devices, such as servers and storage, to the network. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring efficient data flow and network performance within data center environments.</p></p>
