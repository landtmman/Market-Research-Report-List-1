<p><strong>The EMT Pipe Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw Fixed</li><li>Spinning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMT pipe joint market primarily encompasses two types: screw fixed and spinning. Screw fixed joints involve mechanical fastening using screws or bolts, ensuring a secure connection for electrical conduits. This type facilitates ease of assembly and disassembly, making it popular for temporary installations. On the other hand, spinning joints utilize a welding technique that creates a continuous and robust bond. This method is favored for its durability and strength, suitable for long-term applications in various industrial settings.</p></p>
