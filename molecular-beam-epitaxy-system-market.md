<p><ul><li>Normal MBE</li><li>Laser MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) system market includes two main types: Normal MBE and Laser MBE. Normal MBE involves the deposition of thin films through the direct thermal evaporation of materials in a high vacuum, allowing precise control over layer thickness and composition. In contrast, Laser MBE uses lasers to desorb material from a target, improving the efficiency of film growth and enabling the deposition of complex materials. Both technologies cater to advanced semiconductor and optoelectronic applications.</p></p>
