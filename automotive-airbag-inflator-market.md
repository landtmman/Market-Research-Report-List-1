<p><strong>The Automotive Airbag Inflator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Gas Inflator</li><li>Hybrid Gas Inflator</li><li>Stored Gas Inflator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive airbag inflator market consists of three primary types: **pyrotechnic gas inflators**, which utilize a chemical reaction to produce gas rapidly during a collision; **hybrid gas inflators**, which combine both pyrotechnic and compressed gas methods for increased efficiency and reliability; and **stored gas inflators**, which use pre-compressed gas that is released when triggered. Each type varies in deployment speed, reliability, and complexity, catering to specific safety requirements and design preferences in automotive applications.</p></p>
