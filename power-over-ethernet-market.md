<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) is a technology that allows electrical power to be transmitted along with data over standard Ethernet cabling. This reduces the need for additional power sources, making installation easier and more cost-effective. The rise of IoT applications is also driving the adoption of PoE, as it simplifies infrastructure and enhances energy efficiency. Additionally, the implementation of smart city initiatives is boosting the integration of PoE technology in urban environments. As businesses continue to seek cost-effective and efficient solutions for network infrastructure, the market for Power over Ethernet is expected to expand, supported by technological innovations and growing awareness of its benefits across various sectors.</p></p>
