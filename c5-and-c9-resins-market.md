<p><p>C5 and C9 resins are types of hydrocarbon resins derived from the fractionation of petroleum or natural sources. C5 resins are primarily based on pentameric structures and are known for their low molecular weight, excellent adhesive properties, and compatibility with various polymers. They are widely utilized in applications such as adhesives, coatings, inks, and sealants. C9 resins, on the other hand, are derived from heavier fractions and possess higher molecular weights, offering stronger thermal stability and increased durability, making them suitable for more demanding applications.</p><p>The C5 and C9 Resins Market is projected to witness significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
